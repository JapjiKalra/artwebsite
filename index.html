Paper Quillilg

Paper Quillilg is an interesting art of curling a strip of paper, using a needle to form a 3D object. Just like other forms of art it is quite versatile. The paper strip can be fringed , crimped or even moulded into a hemisphere.

I started painting when i was 12 and levelled up to graphic designing at 19(when every thing came to a stand standstill during the lockdown). I'm a certified artist practicing quiet versatile artforms ranging from sketching, painting, animation, illustrating , doodling , quilling to photography. My process focuses more on experimentation and intuition than technique.
